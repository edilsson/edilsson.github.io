About Myself

I was born in 1991, when Chile returned to democracy and there was still a hangover throughout the country from obtaining the Copa Libertadores (Vamos Colo Colo!).

I am a very dedicated, hardworking and proactive person who always tries to give 100% at every task. I try to be a better person every day and to learn from those who are more experienced.

When I was a kid, I was happy just by playing football on the street with my friends or playing PC games like Need For Speed, Quake, Age of Empires, and many more all day.

Over the years I stayed away from games and was more interested in different things related to this area: video editing, mixing music, PC formatting, install software, photo editing, etc. That way, I was never bored on my PC, mostly because I always tried to do something new for me, and I always liked that challenge, so, at the end of the day, computer engineering was the obvious choice.

I love football (watching and playing, although I'm not very good on this last one), having BBQs with friends & family, go to the movies, and hopefully on the future, begin my own adventure with my family.

So, that's all about me that I can say right now! If you feel the need of knowing more about me, why don't contact me?
